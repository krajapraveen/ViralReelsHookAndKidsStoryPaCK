**Key Technical Concepts**
-   **Durable Multi-Stage Pipeline:** Breaking a long-running job into smaller, independent, and persistent stages. Each stage's completion is recorded before the next one begins, allowing for retries and resumption.
-   **Permanent Asset Model:** Treating every generated output as a permanent, user-owned asset stored in object storage (R2) and tracked in a  collection, rather than a temporary file.
-   **Storage-First Principle:** The principle that an asset must be successfully uploaded and validated in storage *before* its corresponding record is created in the database and exposed to the user.
-   **Tier-Aware Degradation & Queue Prioritization:** The architectural pattern of managing system resources under load by providing different levels of service and priority based on user subscription tiers.
-   **Cost Guardrails:** A protective layer to prevent uncontrolled spending by enforcing hard limits on resource consumption at the job, user, and system levels.
