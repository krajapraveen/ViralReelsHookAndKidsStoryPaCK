## 🎬 Feature 1: AI Reel Script Generator

### Step-by-Step Guide:
1. From Dashboard, click **"Create Viral Reel"** card
2. Or navigate to `/app/reels`

### Generating a Reel Script:
1. **Select Niche** - Choose from:
   - Luxury, Relationship, Health, Motivation
   - Parenting, Business, Travel, Food
   
2. **Select Format** - Choose style:
   - Story Time, Hot Take, Tutorial
   - Before/After, Day in Life, etc.

3. **Enter Topic** (Optional)
   - Add specific topic or leave blank for AI suggestion
   
4. Click **"Generate Viral Reel"** (1 credit)

### What You Get:
- **5 Hook Options** - Attention-grabbing opening lines
- **Best Hook** - AI-recommended top performer
- **Full Script** - Scene-by-scene breakdown with:
  - Visual directions
  - Dialogue/narration
  - Timing suggestions
- **Captions** - Ready-to-copy text
- **20 Hashtags** - Trending and niche-specific
- **Posting Tips** - Best times and strategies

### Saving Your Work:
- Click **"Download Script"** to save as JSON
- Click **"Copy"** buttons to copy individual sections
- View past generations in **History** page

## 📖 Feature 2: Kids Story Generator

### Step-by-Step Guide:
1. From Dashboard, click **"Create Kids Story"** card
2. Or navigate to `/app/stories`

### Generating a Story:
1. **Select Age Group**:
   - 4-6 years (Preschool)
   - 6-8 years (Early Reader)
   - 8-10 years (Middle Reader)
   - 10-13 years (Tween)
   - 13-15 years (Early Teen)
   - 15-17 years (Late Teen)

2. **Select Genre**:
   - Adventure, Fantasy, Friendship
   - Animal, Educational, Custom

3. **Enter Character Name** (Optional)
   - Default hero name or your choice

4. Click **"Generate Story Pack"** (6-10 credits)

### What You Get:
- **Story Title & Synopsis**
- **Character Profiles** - Names, roles, descriptions
- **5 Scenes** with:
  - Scene title and setting
  - Full narration
  - Character dialogue
- **Moral of the Story**

### Educational Add-ons:

#### A. Story Worksheet (3 credits)
1. After generating a story, scroll to **"Educational Add-ons"**
2. Click **"Generate"** under Story Worksheet
3. You'll get:
   - **Fill in the Blanks** - Interactive questions
   - **Vocabulary Words** - Key terms with meanings
   - **Comprehension Questions** - Understanding check
   - **Moral Reflection** - Discussion prompts
   - **Drawing Activity** - Creative prompt

4. **Interactive Mode**:
   - Type answers in the input fields
   - Click **"Check My Answers"** for instant feedback
   - Green ✓ = Correct, Red ✗ = Incorrect
   - Click **"Show Answers"** to reveal correct answers

#### B. Printable Story Book PDF (4-6 credits)
1. Click **"Create PDF"** under Printable Story Book
2. **Personalization Pack** (Optional, +2 credits):
   - Check the **"Personalization Pack"** checkbox
   - Enter **Child's Name** (replaces hero name)
   - Enter **Dedication Message** (appears on dedication page)
   - Enter **Birthday Message** (optional)
3. Click **"Create PDF"**
4. PDF automatically downloads with:
   - Colorful cover page with genre image
   - Dedication page (if personalized)
   - Character page with avatars
   - Scene pages with illustrations
   - Moral page with nature image
   - Activity page with cute animals
   - "The End" page
   - **CreatorStudio AI watermark** on all pages

## 🛠️ Feature 3: Creator Tools

### Accessing Creator Tools:
1. From Dashboard, click **"Creator Tools"** card
2. Or navigate to `/app/creator-tools`

### Tab 1: 30-Day Content Calendar
1. Click **"Calendar"** tab
2. Select your **Niche** (e.g., Business, Health)
3. Choose **Number of Days** (7, 14, 30)
4. Toggle **"Include full scripts"** for detailed scripts (+15 credits)
5. Click **"Generate Calendar"** (10-25 credits)
6. **Result**: Daily content ideas with:
   - Day number
   - Content topic
   - Suggested format
   - Full script (if selected)
7. Click **"Download Calendar"** to save

### Tab 2: Carousel Generator
1. Click **"Carousel"** tab
2. Select your **Niche**
3. Enter your **Topic** (e.g., "5 morning habits for success")
4. Choose **Number of Slides** (5-10)
5. Click **"Generate Carousel"** (2 credits)
6. **Result**: Multi-slide content with:
   - Slide headlines
   - Body text
   - Call-to-action for last slide
7. Click **"Copy All"** or copy individual slides

### Tab 3: Hashtag Bank
1. Click **"Hashtags"** tab
2. Select your **Niche**
3. Click **"Get Hashtags"** (FREE)
4. **Result**: 30 curated hashtags:
   - 10 High Volume (1M+ posts)
   - 10 Medium Volume (100K-1M)
   - 10 Niche Specific (<100K)
5. Click **"Copy All"** to copy

### Tab 4: Thumbnail Text Generator
1. Click **"Thumbnails"** tab
2. Enter your **Video Topic**
3. Select **Style** (Curiosity, Benefit, Question, etc.)
4. Click **"Generate Headlines"** (FREE)
5. **Result**: 5 headline options
6. Click to copy any headline

### Tab 5: Trending Topics
1. Click **"Trending"** tab
2. View weekly trending content topics
3. Each topic shows:
   - Title and niche
   - Hook preview
   - Suggested angle
4. Use these for content inspiration

### Tab 6: Convert Content
1. Click **"Convert"** tab
2. Choose conversion type:

   **Reel → Carousel** (1 credit)
   - Select a reel from dropdown
   - Click "Convert"
   - Get carousel slides from your reel script

   **Reel → YouTube** (2 credits)
   - Select a reel to expand
   - Click "Expand"
   - Get 8-10 minute YouTube script

   **Story → Reel** (1 credit)
   - Select a kids story
   - Click "Convert"
   - Get parenting reel about the moral

   **Story → Quote** (FREE)
   - Select a story
   - Click "Generate"
   - Get shareable moral quotes
